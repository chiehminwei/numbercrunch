Common questions

What software do you work with?
Any software that can export a CSV or Excel file — QuickBooks, Xero, Sage, NetSuite, FreshBooks, or custom ERPs. We work from exports, not live connections. No read-only access to your software required.
Is my client data safe?
We process the file you send and return the deliverable by email. We don't store files after delivery, don't request software access, and don't share data with third parties. NDAs available on request.
What if the output is wrong?
Reply and we'll fix it same day. Every workpaper is delivered as an editable Excel file with visible formulas — you can check every number yourself. Structural errors are corrected at no charge.
Do you handle tax returns, audits, or compilations?
No. We focus on the mechanical pre-work that supports those engagements: reconciliations, close workpapers, variance reports, audit sampling. We're not a licensed CPA firm and don't produce signed work product.
What's the difference between standard and complex?
Standard ($149) covers single-account, single-period tasks under ~300 transactions. Complex ($299) is for multi-account reconciliations, large files (500+ transactions), multiple periods, or custom output requirements. Not sure which applies? Just email and describe the task — we'll tell you.
How do I pay?
We invoice after delivery for single tasks. Monthly plans are via Stripe — reply "monthly" to any email and we'll send the setup link. No credit card required to send your first task.
